Privacy Policy

Ghosted Necrometer ("the app") is an entertainment application. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Ghosted Necrometer does not collect, store, or share personal data. No user accounts are required.

Camera and Microphone

Camera and microphone access are used exclusively for real-time visual and audio effects within the app. No images, videos, or audio recordings are saved, transmitted, or stored on any server.

Device Sensors

The app reads device sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer) to generate simulated readings. Sensor data is processed locally and is never transmitted or stored beyond the current session.

Local Storage

The app uses on-device local storage to save user preferences and session data. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Analytics and Tracking

No analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services are used.

Network Usage

The app may connect to external services for optional AI-generated content. No personal data is included in these requests.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Entertainment Disclaimer

This application is intended for entertainment purposes only. It does not detect ghosts, spirits, or paranormal activity. All readings and effects are simulated.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
